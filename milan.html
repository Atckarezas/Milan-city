Transportation in Milan

Milan subways run frequently and even every few minutes during rush hour.

  • You can buy Milan metro tickets in the metro stations at an ATM ticket machine.
  • A standard one-way ticket costs 2.20 euros.
  • Tickets are valid for 90 minutes on the metro, trams and buses after stamping.
